F-25 · Customer Orders & Tracking · High-Fidelity Mockup #1

What /orders — “Đơn hàng & Theo dõi” — looks like

The merged customer post-order screen at iPhone width (390 px): five zones on one scroll — sticky top bar + live pill · active-order banner · the summary ⇄ detail centerpiece · your order history · the whole-floor live queue — over the shell bottom nav. Rendered with the canonical worked example: Bàn 04 · 103.000 đ · 7 loại. The four small frames on the right are the states you can’t see at the same time as the main one: the two overlays, the SSE-down branch and the no-active-order branch.

Traced to customer_orders_tracking_PLAN.md §4.4 (the 15 behaviors) · §4.1 (components) · §4.3 (the six render branches), and to the screen’s DESIGN_PROMPT.md for layout order and Vietnamese copy. Palette is the reference dark/orange customer shell (hard-coded hexes, not theme tokens, not the F-7 neutral set) so the sheet is identical in light and dark viewers. Money glyph is đ per PLAN §7 — the one app-wide formatVND(), not the DESIGN_PROMPT’s ₫.

What the mockup pins down §4.4

  • One stream, four surfaces — the pill, the banner badge + #2 trong 5 đơn/ETA, per-dish progress, and the floor queue all come from the single useOrderMonitorSSE. Position and ETA are BE-computed, so the banner and zone 5 can never disagree.
  • Read-only placed order — quantities are ×n badges, never steppers; 🗑 is replaced by Huỷ, shown only while cancel is allowed; GHI CHÚ is read-only with ✓ Đã lưu.
  • The rollup is the math proof — 7 loại after merging on dish name AND nhân set: Canh có rau ×7 (1 + 2 from the two suất + 4 individual) collapses to one row, while Bánh Trứng Vàng stays separate from the combos’ Bánh Trứng Chín. Combo header rows (0 đ) never appear.
  • Branch 2 and branch 5 are non-fatal — losing your order or the stream never takes the history and the floor queue down with it. Only branch 1 (session expired) is full-screen.

Deliberate deviations from the DESIGN_PROMPT §7

  • Money glyph đ, not ₫ — the DESIGN_PROMPT asks for 105.000 ₫; the plan default is the menu page’s glyph through the one app-wide formatVND(). Zero-priced canh renders 0 đ, never blank.
  • Worked example is Bàn 04 / 103.000 đ everywhere in zones 2–3, so this page’s receipts match the menu plan’s canonical order. The 105.000 đ figure survives only as the Bàn 03 history card — a different order.
  • Queue position/ETA are rendered, not computed — the DESIGN_PROMPT’s position = idx+1 / ETA = idx×3 recipe is exactly the reference bug the plan designs out.
  • No quantity stepper and one detail component (no overlay twin) — §3.4.
  • Cancel rule still open — the buttons here assume the owner’s “cancel any time before payment”; the reference’s 30 %-served gate is the alternative. One canCancel predicate, one-line flip.
⚠️ FLAG — two numbers to lock before the O-3 build. (1) The cancel rule (owner’s “any time before payment” vs the reference’s < 30 % served) — the BE gate and this button must never disagree. (2) The money glyph — this sheet ships đ; say the word and the one formatVND() flips everywhere at once.
