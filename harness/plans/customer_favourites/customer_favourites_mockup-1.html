What /menu/favourites will look like

A high-fidelity, interactive MOCKUP (not the build) of the favourites hub — the C-6 preview the owner asked to eyeball before implementation. Everything here is clickable: the segmented tabs switch views, the filter chips filter, the hearts remove cards, the steppers recompute the live total, and the builder prices itself. Colors are the locked customer shell (#0b0f17 + #f97316) hard-coded, so this page looks the same in a light or dark viewer theme.

What you are looking at

C-6 (v1) — everything without a badge

  • Resolve + auto-prune → the dismissible toast at first paint (§4.4-2).
  • Filter chips with live counts, active = solid orange (§4.4-3).
  • Card = the remove control: the filled orange ♥ un-favourites and removes the card; the stepper edits the line (§4.4-4).
  • Bulk add: 🛒 Thêm tất cả vào giỏ, additive (§4.4-5).
  • Bộ đã lưu: apply / rename / delete (§4.4-7).
  • Empty state — remove all three cards to see it; "Xem Menu" restores the seed here.

C-7 (deferred) — everything badged 💡 ĐỀ XUẤT / C-7

  • Canh quick-add block (two 0 đ rows, ✓ Đã thêm flash).
  • The live-total row above the CTA.
  • The whole "Tự tạo suất" builder (suats[] + per-egg nhân + free-text note).
  • 📌 Ghim lên Menu pinning (set cards + the save modal checkbox).

Reference bug fixed here

  • The footer / CTA reserves 72px of bottom padding and sits above the fixed 5-tab nav — never behind it (§4.4-8, §6). Scroll view A to the bottom to check.

⚠️ FLAG — this is a mockup, not the build.
